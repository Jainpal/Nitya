  • They have a perference foe one side od their body
  • Poor posture like W sitting or slouched posture
  • Baby seems floppy and weak, or stif and inflexible
  • Balance and coordination concerns
  • Poor quality movements ( wobby, jerky, shaky or uncontrolled).
  • Trouble progressing gross motor skills and motor Development
  • Improving physical activity, participating and tolerance.
  • Improving functional mobility.
  • Difficult judging the force, direction or timing of movements.
