About Us

The Beginning

Established in 2003 by Vivek Ray, a Speech & Language Pathologist with close to 30 years of experience, Nitya The Hope (previously known as Nitya) is an organisation that helps bridge the gaps between children on the Autism Spectrum and the world around them. Our intervention program has been the first-of-its-kind in Ahmedabad to offer an interdisciplinary strategy comprising Speech Therapy, Occupational Therapy and Pre-Academic Inputs, all under one roof.

Our program has consistently demonstrated outstanding results. Our team is always adapting and improving its methods to make them as effective as they can be.

Our Approach

The better we are at expressing ourselves, the better we connect with others!

We work with children to create an enabling and empowering environment. This helps them put their unique experiences into words and become adept at communicating their perspectives and needs.

With time and patience, we try to identify each child’s strengths and help them optimise their full potential.
